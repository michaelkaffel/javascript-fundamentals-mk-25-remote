  • When you click Roll Dice!, you will roll up to 10 dice, one at a time.
  • If you roll a 1, game stops and all further rolls are forfeited.
  • If you roll 2 or 3, you win nothing but roll again.
  • If you roll a 4, you lose a point (if you have one) but roll again.
  • If you roll a 5, you win 5 gold coins and roll again.
  • If you roll a 6, you win 6 gold coins and roll again.
